ABOUT ABHIVYAKTI

Abhivyakti is a stage for artists from different genres and background to meet, understand and appreciate other artists and their creations, engaging different generations under a single roof - for a single cause brings them together. The messages portrayed by these fabulous artists through their brushes brings us big hopes for the future. The special thing about our event is that we do not restrict our audience by age or by a single art form; we give them the liberty to create and surprise us in any way.
