I am Preeti Das. I’m a btech undergraduate at Delhi Technological University. Throughout my life, art has been my escape from the world. When words fail, my art delivers my thoughts and imagination. I usually use acrylic paints, poster paints, and dry pastels for my artworks. I have recently started exploring digital art as well.
