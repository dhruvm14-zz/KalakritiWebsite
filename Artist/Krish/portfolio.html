Krish Saini
sainisketchings Krish Saini

I am a traditional artist who has a special bond with pencils and paper. I am improvising in hyper-realistic portraits. I do commission portraits and also make tutorials for beginner artists. My art pages and channel are named as “sainisketchings”. Since childhood I had a passion of drawing. Unlike others I don’t like binge-watching, rather I would prefer to spend my whole night working over an art-piece. When I was a kid, I kept my sketchbook with me all the time and drew in it whenever I found time, be it be school, tuition or even when visiting a relative XD. Then I participated in many competitions in school and even won plenty of certificates and prizes; in 6th my art got selected in top 10 by UNESCO. Then that passion faded with the pressure of studies. On getting into college, I woke up my skills and joined KALAKRITI-Creative Art Society of DTU and also created my art page. These two steps pushed me to follow my passion. In lockdown I got plenty of time to improve my skills and since then I am drawing back to back artworks and have improved immensely. Now I am a Art Mentor in Kalakriti and guiding others to follow their passion. This was the journey till now, still a long way to go…
