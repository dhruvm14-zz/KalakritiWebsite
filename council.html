And now that I'm one of the shortlisted candidates for the position of Secretary, I feel really obliged. I've always tried my best and gave my best for the betterment of society. Be it sharing my views and ideas for any event that society is preparing to organize, I'd always be enthusiastic and always be for the same. For a long time, I've been eager to improve my managing skills, and being the PR Co-head undoubtedly helped a lot in that aspect. But now, I'd like to put all those skills that I've honed in the past year into work. There were situations where I did some things wrong. I took responsibility and rectified those mistakes. Also, I'm the kind of person who works systematically, so becoming the Secretary, this habit of mine will surely come out in handy. Also, I'd like to add that I'm really passionate about my work and would even skip my sleep if the work is not yet complete. I'd try to complete it today or as soon as possible but not procrastinate. I remember one time creating content for the page even though I had tests non-stop. I am dedicated to my work and a hard worker. I forgot to mention that I'm always looking for abstract ideas for kinds of stuff here and there. And that's how I stand unique from the others. You can simply do the same thing, but why not try something new maybe it would turn out good. Perhaps not, but you'll surely gain the experience. There's no negative in that. People often don't like my ideas, but that negative feedback makes me even more motivated so that I can get their approval even after trying maybe ten times, and then maybe 100 times, and so on. But that won't make me falter. I'll still give my 100 percent. With that, I'd conclude this SOP. And even if I'm not selected for the position, I'd always give my best wishes to the one who does get selected. And I'd like to improve those things which I lacked and will work on them. But if I do get selected, I would give the position my all to the best of my abilities. With all that "Always Keep Smiling and keep on grooming yourself that'd even make your past self proud." Thank You
